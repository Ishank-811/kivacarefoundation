Changing Lives, One Paw at a Time.


At KIVA CARE FOUNDATION, we’re dedicated to rescuing and caring for abandoned, injured, and sick animals. With your help, we’re giving these animals a second chance at life through rescue, fostering, and rehoming programs.

Together, we can make a difference in their lives. Join us in our mission to bring hope and care to those who need it most.

How You Can Help

There are countless ways you can help us in our quest for paws. You can make a donation (however big or small), join our fundraiser events, or volunteer your time and home as a foster parent.

How We Help

Animal Rescue

We rescue street dogs and other animals, doing our best to provide them with shelter and care.
